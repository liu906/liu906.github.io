About me

Hi there! I'm Xutong, a postdoctoral researcher affiliated with TU Delft, the University of Twente, and ING Analytics. My research lies at the intersection of software engineering and AI, with a focus on building reliable, efficient, and sustainable systems.

My current work spans green software engineering and the reliability of Large Language Models (LLMs), where I study robustness, consistency, and performance of AI systems in real-world applications. I am particularly interested in understanding how LLM-based tools impact software development practices and how to evaluate them rigorously.

Before this, I completed my Ph.D. at Nanjing University under the supervision of Professor Yuming Zhou, focusing on software quality assurance, including defect prediction and testing evaluation.
