About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Software Engineering Research Group at TU Delft. My research focuses on software quality assurance, specifically in software testing and defect prediction. I received my Ph.D. from Nanjing University, where my thesis was titled "Towards a reliable evaluation of actual effectiveness of defect prediction models," supervised by Professor Yuming Zhou.
